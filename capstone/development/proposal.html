Proposal

Topic

My topic is a photo database/gallery of interesting or cool bikes throughout Davis to celebrate the city's long history with bikes. The target audience for my project will be adults, 18+.

Partner

I don't plan to work with a partner.

Features

To make my project interactive and dynamic, I plan to have a feature where users can upload their own photos to the database to contribute. I might also consider implementing a "liking" system where photos that are more liked are brought to the top.

Vague Ideas

I'm not sure yet about my project's layout, but I do want the photos to be the central focus. I'd like to have a gallery where photos have different sizes and fit together like puzzle pieces, but I don't know any easy way to actually implement that.
